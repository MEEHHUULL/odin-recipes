Pizza

Pizza - soft doughy bread and gooey mozzarella cheese

Ingredients
  • Pizza base
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • mixed herbs
Method
  1. preheat oven at 200 degrees c
  2. make suace with tomatoes
  3. spread sauce on pizza base
  4. sprinkle cheese on top
  5. cook in oven for 12-25 minutes until crispy
